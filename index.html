What is HouseBite?

A mobile app that turns your household into a smart pantry — less waste, less spending, less stress.

You join or create a household, and HouseBite becomes your shared food manager. Add products with expiry dates, get notified before they go bad, and receive recipe suggestions that use exactly what you already have at home.

Beyond the pantry, it tracks grocery spending, lets members scan receipts to log purchases automatically, and handles dietary restrictions per person — so no one's left out of the meal plan.

30–40% of food bought in Europe is wasted at household level
€600 average annual cost of food waste per European household
What it does

Built for real households

⏰

Expiry tracking & recipe suggestions

Every product is stored with its expiry date. When something is about to expire, HouseBite surfaces recipes that use it — so you cook it before you lose it.

🛒

Shared shopping list

All household members share one grocery list. Add items, tick them off at the store — synced in real time.

📄

Receipt scanning

Photograph a grocery receipt and HouseBite logs the items and total cost automatically. No manual entry.

💰

Budget tracking

Set a monthly grocery budget and watch spending in real time — by member, by category, or for the whole household at once.

🏠

Household management

Create a household and invite others via a unique code or link. Everyone sees the same pantry.

🚫

Dietary restrictions

Set allergies and intolerances per member. HouseBite flags restricted ingredients in recipes and shopping suggestions.
